# Sound: XM4 Weapon Fire

## Sound Description
- **Type:** SFX
- **Context:** Plays each time the XM4 assault rifle is fired. The XM4 is a futuristic bullpup assault rifle with a high fire rate of 730 RPM.
- **Emotional Quality:** Polished, futuristic, authoritative — conveys next-generation military technology with surgical precision.
- **Duration:** 0.10s per shot
- **Tonal Characterization:** A smooth, controlled crack that sits between the M4A1's sharpness and the AK-47's depth. The XM4's report has a distinctive mechanical refinement — the sound is cleaner, less harsh, with a subtle electronic-pneumatic undertone suggesting advanced recoil mitigation and a compensator system. The muzzle crack is tight and directional with a slight metallic "ting" resonance from the bullpup chassis. The supersonic projectile produces a crisp whip-crack that trails off quickly. In full-auto, the XM4 produces a rapid, almost rhythmic series of controlled pops — each round precisely spaced, suggesting a weapon system with minimal muzzle rise and exceptional stability.

## Layering Notes
- **Attack:** Fast, clean transient — less harsh than traditional assault rifles
- **Sustain:** Very brief (8-15ms) — the refined crack of a compensated barrel
- **Decay:** Short and clean — 60-80ms, minimal ringing
- **Reverb:** Controlled — the compensator reduces muzzle report echo
- **Distance modeling:** Sharper roll-off at range due to the higher fire rate and lower individual shot volume
